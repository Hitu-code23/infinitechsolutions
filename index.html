01 — Philosophy 一

Master of
image and shadow

4 Years of Steel
37 Films Forged
12 Studios

I am Hitin Banga — a self-taught director drawn to the silence between frames. Like the samurai who finds truth in stillness before the strike, I seek the precise moment when story becomes emotion.

Four years spent at Parallax, Zenith, and the frontiers of independent cinema have forged a singular belief: that the most powerful images are those carved from restraint — not abundance. Every cut is a decision. Every frame, a commitment.

My practice spans visual effects, narrative architecture, and the invisible craft of rhythm — the tension a samurai holds before the blade moves. Digital artifacts can possess the soul of celluloid. This is the dōjō where that truth is tested.
